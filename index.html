Welcome to the Broken Bayou Homeowners Association

A well established neighborhood nestled in West Houston

Just off Memorial Drive, between Eldridge & Highway 6, Broken Bayou features twenty townhomes ranging in size from 2,500 – 4,600 sq. ft., originally constructed in the late 1970's. This gated community includes walking trails, a pool, and a tennis court for residents with security lighting throughout the complex. Located on a peaceful ravine, Broken Bayou provides private secure access for residents and private parking for visitors on site.
